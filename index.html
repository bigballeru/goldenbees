Our Caribbean Story

Golden Bees began as a family venture in 2022, when we started sharing our treasured Caribbean recipes from our home kitchen. With no storefront, we offered pickup and delivery services as word of our authentic flavors spread throughout Provo.

The enthusiastic response from our community allowed us to grow quickly, and in 2025 we proudly opened our restaurant on University Avenue. As a family-owned business, our recipes have been passed down through generations, carrying the vibrant flavors and traditions of the Caribbean.

We pour our hearts into every dish we serve, using fresh ingredients to create authentic experiences that transport you to the islands with every bite. When you dine with us, you're not just a customer — you're part of our extended family.
